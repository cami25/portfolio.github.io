Bestie Goals:

Friends who share the same sneaker game are winning May 21st, 2021

Finally taking in the W Lets be real, buying sneakers now a days is like winning the lottery. With so many bots and people flipping them for profit, buying sneakers isnt what it use to be back when were younger. Probably out of every 50 shoes I try to purchase I wind up only getting luck once. Apps like the SNKRS app dont have a system to crack down on bots, which makes it nearly impossible to buy the shoes you want at the retail price. I was very lucky when it came to snagging a pair of the silver toe jordan 1s for $170 which has a resell value of $375
